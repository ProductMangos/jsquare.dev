Why a website and this design?

Dec 20, 2024

As I continue to develop my skills and knowledge in the field of software development, I have come to realize that I am absolutely terrible at remembering what I've done what to a certain point. My weakness when it comes to development is my lack of motivation and organization to document the things I've tried and/or completed.

I'm hoping to use this website to provide a platform for me to share my thoughts and ideas on various topics and, since this is my personal website that I'm actually going to share with others, I have a belief that this may (or not) keep me accountable.

Why this design?

I am not a very creative designer when it comes to the appearance of websites so I like to take a lot of inspiration from other existing websites. One website in particular I took much inspiration from: Cassidy Williams

I really enjoy her minimalistic design without the need to go over the top with CSS splashes and animations. I like to keep things simple and clean and her website is a great example of that. I took what I considered the main points of the website and applied them to my own website without the wide various of separate and uniquely changing colors.

One other key difference between our websites is also how it's built, which probably describes me best... I wanted to create a website with a known design but do it myself differently than the original. This website was create with just HTML, CSS and vanilla JS using AJAX oppose to using a framework.

Closing

For those that would actually read these blogs from my very minimal website, I hope you enjoy it and find it useful. Cheers.
